Emergencies Assistance Information

    Complex Crises

    Thousands of people everyday are facing life-threatening situations. UNHCR is currently responding to major conflicts and provided life-saving assistance to those in need.

    Explore more about the current situations.

    Increase in population displacement

    Over the past 2 years a series of emergencies have forced more people to flee across borders than at any time in the last decade.

    Relief Assistance Distribution

    UNHCR responds to emergencies immediately and begins delivering life-saving assistance. On the ground, UNHCR is distributing unprecedented numbers of tents, food, and other life-saving supplies.

    Emergency Staff Deployments

    In the midst of the response, local and international staff are deployed to the emergency regions to assist those in need.

    Finances Needed to Assist

    Support is needed to continue to provide assistance to those in need. Currently, the total need to respond effectively is unfulfilled. Underfunding continues to delay response and put lives at risk.
